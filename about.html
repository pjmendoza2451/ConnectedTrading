About Me

I am a seasoned forex trader with over six years of experience in the industry. As a trading speaker at Octa, formerly known as OctaFX, I have had the privilege of sharing my knowledge and strategies with a wide audience. Over the years, I have been invited by various companies and groups to teach forex trading, helping countless individuals to master the skills needed to succeed in the market.

My teaching journey has enabled me to mentor hundreds, if not thousands, of students, guiding them through the complexities of forex trading. I have also had the honor of assisting a startup trading desk, providing them with the insights and tools necessary for success.

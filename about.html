About Me

I started trading forex with high hopes… and lost everything in just 2 days. It was a harsh wake-up call, but it taught me the most important lesson: trading is a skill, not luck.

From those early struggles, I dedicated myself to learning everything from proper risk management to strategy development. Soon, I was not just trading my own account, but teaching others how to do it responsibly. I even became a trading speaker at Octa (formerly OctaFX), sharing insights and guiding new traders.

Today, I’m proud to say I’m a funded trader at Alpha Capital, consistently applying strategies I’ve mastered and mentoring students to trade with confidence.

If you want to avoid the mistakes I made and trade like a pro, I’m here to guide you every step of the way.
